The Problem

Thousands of bikes are stolen in Israel every year. Owners have no centralized platform to report theft, share information, or alert others—making recovery nearly impossible.

The Solution

A community-powered platform where victims can quickly report stolen bikes with photos, descriptions, and location data. The searchable database and real-time alerts help the community spot and recover stolen vehicles.

The Impact

By creating visibility around bike theft, we've built a deterrent system and support network. Recovered bikes mean real wins for real people—and every success story strengthens the community.

Platform Overview

A comprehensive platform for browsing reports, filing new cases, and tracking recoveries across Israel.

Stolen Bikes Database

Browse all reported stolen bikes with detailed information, photos, and location data to help identify and recover them.

How It Works

Four simple steps to report your stolen bike, alert the community, and maximize your chances of recovery.

Admin Dashboard

Powerful administrative tools for managing reports, analyzing theft patterns, and coordinating with authorities.

Official Partnership

Proud cooperation with the Tel Aviv-Yafo Municipality to combat bike theft in the city.

Key Features

Quick Reporting

File a report in under 2 minutes. Upload photos, add descriptions, mark the location, and your stolen bike is instantly visible to the entire community.

Interactive Map

Visual heat map showing theft hotspots across Israel. See where bikes were stolen, identify risky areas, and help the community stay vigilant.

Real-Time Alerts

Get notified when bikes matching your search criteria are reported, or when there's theft activity in your neighborhood. Stay informed, stay protected.

Smart Search

Search by brand, color, type, serial number, or location. Powerful filters help you scan through reports quickly to spot your bike or help others.

Community Power

Crowdsourced recovery network. When someone spots a bike, they can flag it. The more eyes we have, the better our chances of reuniting owners with their bikes.

Prevention Tips

Educational resources on securing your bike, choosing quality locks, safe parking spots, and what to do immediately after a theft to maximize recovery chances.
