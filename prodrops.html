ProDrops.gg is a digital card collecting platform featuring cards of popular esports players and streamers. Users can collect, trade, and battle with these cards, engaging in strategic gameplay within a digital card game environment.

The platform aims to provide a unique and immersive experience for fans of esports and digital collectibles. Over two years of work includes 2D/3D assets, design systems, and marketing materials.
